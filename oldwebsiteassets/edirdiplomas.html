About EDiR Diplomas

myEDiR.net offers a wide selection of individual self-assessment modules so you can get acquainted with the different testing types such as Multiple Response Questions, Short Cases and Clinically Oriented Reasoning Evaluation (CORE) Cases. Each module has been designed to perfectly match the curriculum of both the EDiR and the European Radiology Associations Diploma examinations. If you simply want to focus on a particular topic or prepare for a specific diploma, myEDiR.net aims to be a cost effective and efficient tool in helping candidates pass the exam.
