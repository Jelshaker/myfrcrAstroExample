What is myRCPSC?

myRCPSC is the most comprehensive and extensive revision resource available for the Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada radiology exam, offered both online and in print. The unprecedented scale of the database allows for complete coverage of the RCPSC curriculum across all subspecialties and exam components. Thousands of high-quality image studies are integrated into a vast array of clinically-oriented cases and rapid-fire sessions, ensuring you are prepared for both common presentations and the rare, complex cases that test your clinical acumen.

Clinically Oriented Reasoning Evaluation (CORE) Cases

The CORE cases serve as the backbone of the platform's RCPSC preparation, simulating the high difficulty and critical components of the examination. These cases are designed to test your clinical reasoning through a structured exam delivery—from initial image interpretation and differential diagnosis to the most appropriate final management decisions.

Exam Components

Short Cases

The Short Cases are designed to replicate the intensity and pace of the RCPSC's image-based reporting sessions. These cases focus on the critical skill of making fast, accurate diagnoses from single or limited-series studies, emphasizing high-yield pathology and common exam "pitfalls."

  • Efficient Revision — Full examination packets to cycle through numerous radiographs and cross-sectional images.
  • Skill Refinement — Each case targets specific "must-see" diagnoses, helping you avoid common pitfalls.
  • Performance Benchmarking — Detailed answers and explanations provide clear standards for the required level of detail.
Knowledge Assessment

Multiple Response Questions

The MRQs are designed to test your theoretical knowledge and clinical judgment across the entire RCPSC curriculum. Unlike standard multiple-choice questions, these often require you to identify one or more correct answers, reflecting the complexity of real-world radiological decision-making.

  • Broaden Curriculum Coverage — Complete coverage of core topics including physics, radiation safety, and pharmacology.
  • Simulate Exam Logic — The platform uses the same proportional scoring and question types found in the RCPSC exam.
  • Identify Knowledge Gaps — Instant scoring helps pinpoint specific areas needing further refinement.
